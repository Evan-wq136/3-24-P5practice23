One Piece Game Community

To find a One Piece game community, especially for the Trading Card Game (TCG), use the Bandai TCG+ app to find local game stores (LGS) and official tournaments. Join online communities via Reddit (r/OnePieceTCG), Facebook groups, and Discord for finding webcam games or local meetups.

  • Local Game Stores (LGS): Call local hobby shops to ask if they hold One Piece card tournaments.
  • Bandai TCG+ App: This is the official tool for locating registered stores and signing up for "Store Tournament" and "Release Event" sessions..
  • Online/Social Media:
    • Reddit: Visit r/OnePieceTCG for tips, local event updates, and finding players in your area.
    • Discord: Look for community-run servers, which often offer webcam play and advice.
    • Facebook Groups: Search for local or regional groups (e.g., "Florida One Piece Card Game Community") for trading and local meetups.
  • Online Simulators: For the TCG, many play on optcgsim.

Starting Your Own Local Group

If you cannot find a nearby group, you can build one by connecting with interested players on the r/OnePieceTCG Reddit page, visiting shops that host other Bandai games, or creating a local Facebook group.
