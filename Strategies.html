What You Need to Play the Game

A. Card Types: There are five types of cards including Leader Card, Don!! Card, Character Card, Event Card and Stage Card.

B. Playmat & Card Zones: Including 8 area - Character area, Leader card, Stage card, Deck, Trash, Cost area, Don!! deck and Life cards.

C. How to start a game

  • Shuffle your deck and place your Leader card, Deck, and DON!! deck in their respective zones. Your Leader card should be placed face-up.
  • Do rock-paper-scissors to decide who goes first. The winner chooses to go first or second.
  • Draw 5 cards from your deck. You may mulligan to redraw once if you like.
  • Take cards from the top of your deck equal to your Leader's Life value, and place them face-down in the Life card area.
  • The player going first starts the game.

E. Victory Conditions

  • You win a battle against your opponent's Leader when they have 0 Life cards remaining.
  • Your opponent has no cards left in their deck.
